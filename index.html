What is it?

The Upstate Data Summit is an event that will bring together state and local government officials from across Upstate New York. The event will showcase what leaders are doing to publish open data and to use data to enhance operatsions and inform policy making.

When is it?

The event will be held on Friday, April 13th.

Where will the event be held?

The event will be held in Syracuse with a venue to be determined soon.

Why should I attend?

If you work for a state agency or local government in Upstate and you want to share your work or lessons learned with other government officials, this event will provide an unparalleled opportunity do so. For governments that are just beginning their open data and data analytics programs, the event will provide an opportunity to network and learn from the leaders in these areas fro across Upstate.
